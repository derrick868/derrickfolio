Product Expiry Management System

This custom-built Product Expiry Management System was developed to help retail stores and distributors track the shelf life of perishable goods. It eliminates manual tracking by sending automated notifications before products expire, helping businesses reduce losses and ensure compliance.

The system was developed using PHP (CodeIgniter framework), with a clean Bootstrap UI and a MySQL backend. It also features role-based access, expiry dashboards, and printable reports for inventory auditing.

Project Objectives

  • Enable efficient monitoring of expiry dates for thousands of SKUs
  • Automate alerts for approaching expiry dates
  • Streamline inventory audits and reporting
  • 1
    Automated Notifications

    System sends email/SMS reminders before product expiration dates, reducing waste and liability.

  • 2
    Smart Dashboard

    Intuitive dashboard displays product status by expiry range (e.g. <30 days, <60 days, expired).

  • 3
    Multi-User Roles

    Different access levels for managers, storekeepers, and auditors. Admin control over permissions.

This system has significantly improved operational efficiency for my client by automating a critical inventory management process. It's lightweight, secure, and customizable to suit various business needs across retail, healthcare, and manufacturing.

Delivered Features

  • Expiry Tracking Dashboard with color-coded alerts
  • Custom Reports by product category or expiry range
  • Role-Based User Access for different staff groups
  • Mobile Friendly interface for quick data entry and lookup
